THE EVENTS IN TUBAY CELEBRATED BY TUBAYNONS EVERY YEAR






ARAW NG TUBAY( October 20)

The Araw ng Tubay is Celebrated Every Year by Tubaynons to celebrate the birth of our little town and to honor Datu Tabay, the hero of our town.




VIVA SENIORA SANTA ANA! VIVA SENIOR SAN JOAQUIN( July 26)

Every year, Tubaynons happily celebrates the fiesta of our patron, Sra. Santa Ana, and Sr. San Joaquin.




TUBAY ACTIVE YOUTH ORGANIZATION( June 1-7)

The youth of Tubay Gathers Every Year to to do fun activities and friendly competitions to celebrate their young years in this Town.




TUBAY TAMBAN FESTIVAL( July 17)

The Tubay Tamban Festival celebrated every yeat to celebrate tubay's blessing, which is Tamban.
